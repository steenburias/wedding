Catholic and Filipino Wedding Traditions

Presentation of the Coins
Wedding coins symbolize prosperity and the willingness of the bride and groom to care, sacrifice, provide and contribute to their new life together.

Lighting of the Candle
Unity Candle signifies the union between the couple and their families.

Veil and Cord
Veil symbolizes the union of two into one as well as God's protection and encompassing love for the new couple. The cord symbolizes unity and everlasting devotion.

WHAT IS THE ATTIRE?

Black-tie preferred. Gentlemen wear tuxedos with a bow tie and women wear formal floor-length gowns.

WILL THERE BE AN AFTER-PARTY?

WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE TO THE CHURCH?

The ceremony starts sharply at 2:30pm. We suggest arriving by 2:00 pm to ensure your seating prior to the processional. There will be shuttles leaving Hotel Monaco at 1:45PM and 2PM

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER THE CEREMONY?

The ceremony will be a full Catholic mass, ending at 3:30PM. After the ceremony, the bridal party will be taking pictures around the city before heading to the venue. Cocktail hour begins at 5:30 pm at Union Trust. For others, there are plenty of restaurants and historic sites in the area to check out.

HOW DO I GET TO THE VENUE?

There will be shuttles from the hotels to the venue leaving at 5:00PM and 5:15PM. The venue and church are also close enough to walk. If you are driving, there is a parking garage on 8th and Chestnut street (733 Chestnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19106) which is next door to the venue.

Yes!

WHAT IS UNION TRUST?

Union Trust is a historical bank built in 1888. The couple appreciates its grand architecture, 65 foot illuminated engraved ceilings, and its representation of historic Philadelphia.

ARE CHILDREN ALLOWED?

No.

I'M STAYING AT THE HOTEL, WHERE DO I PARK?

At Hotel Monaco and the Renaissance Hotel, valet parking is available for $55 per night. The recommended alternative parking is at The Bourse Parking Garage at 400 Ranstead Street, $29 for 24 hours. It's a two minute walk from Hotel Monaco.

WHAT ARE THE HOTEL CHECK-IN/OUT TIMES?

At Hotel Monaco, check-in is at 3PM and Check-out is at 12PM. At the Renaissance Hotel, check-in is at 4PM and check-out is at 12PM
