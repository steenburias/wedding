Catholic and Filipino Wedding Traditions

Presentation of the Coins
Wedding coins symbolize prosperity and the willingness of the bride and groom to care, sacrifice, provide and contribute to their new life together.

Lighting of the Candle
Unity Candle signifies the union between the couple and their families.

Veil and Cord
Veil symbolizes the union of two into one as well as God's protection and encompassing love for the new couple. The cord symbolizes unity and everlasting devotion.

WHAT IS UNION TRUST?

Union Trust is a historical bank built in 1888. The couple appreciates its grand architecture, 65 foot illuminated engraved ceilings, and its representation of historic Philadelphia.
