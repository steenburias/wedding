Our Story

The Story Thus Far

It all began with a chance, meeting on the rooftop of Don Tito's in Arlington, VA. Shortly after, Grant & Christine endured two years of coast to coast long-distance. After being 2,400 miles apart for too long, Grant moved to San Francisco to be with his better half.

Over the years, they made great memories together on the beaches of La Jolla, the slopes of Park City, and the islands of the Cyclades. They have enjoyed getting to know and love each other's family and friends, and supporting each other through all of the ups and downs in life. Through it all, they learned to love and respect each other. Their goldendoodle, Bao Bao, was also a huge help!

On a private Hawaiian beach, Grant proposed to Christine. They are excited to fall further in love as they explore all that life has to offer. They look forward to having the honor of family and friends in attendance this fall.
