Philadelphia is known for its cuisine, culture, and colonial history.
The closest airport to Philadelphia is Philadelphia International Airport (PHL). It is about a 20 minute taxi, Uber, Lyft ride to the any of the hotels. SEPTA / Regional rail provides train service between the airport and center city via the Airport Regional Line. There is a regular taxi stand at 30th Street Station along with rideshare apps as well.
